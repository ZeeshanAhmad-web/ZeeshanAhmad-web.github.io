Project

Horizon Forbidden West — Slaughterspine

Personal · Fan Art Infographic · Machine Study 2026

Companion infographic for the Slaughterspine machine — the same studio-style language applied to a much larger silhouette.

The second piece in the series. The challenge here was scale: the silhouette is so much more complex than the Ravager’s that the linework risked drowning the labels. The fix was a tighter grid and more aggressive use of negative space.

Horizon Forbidden West and all related assets are owned by Guerrilla Games and Sony Interactive Entertainment. This piece is fan art, made as a personal study — not endorsed by or affiliated with the rights holders.
