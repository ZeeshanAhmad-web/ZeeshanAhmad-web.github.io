Project

Horizon Forbidden West — Ravager

Personal · Fan Art Infographic · Machine Study 2026

A studio-style infographic breaking down the Ravager machine from Horizon Forbidden West. Fan art; the IP belongs to Guerrilla Games / Sony.

A study in information design — reverse-engineering the visual language of the game’s own internal documentation to see whether I could match its discipline. Cool white linework, controlled accent colours, a grid that does the work of explaining without raising its voice.

Horizon Forbidden West and all related assets are owned by Guerrilla Games and Sony Interactive Entertainment. This piece is fan art, made as a personal study — not endorsed by or affiliated with the rights holders.

Process

Thumbnailing — Initial ideation and composition planning.

Tight Sketch — Refining the anatomy, details, and linework.

Color Comps — Exploring mood, palette, and atmosphere.
