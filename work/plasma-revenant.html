Project

Plasma Revenant

Personal Creature Design 2025

An impossible anatomy holding itself together through sheer voltage — something that should not be standing, standing.

The piece started as a creature design exercise and grew into something stranger — a figure built from absences as much as from bone, with plasma stitched through the gaps where flesh refused to grow. The pose was important: not aggressive, not defensive, just hovering, deciding.

Cool blue-violets carry the entire palette so that the warm pink-white plasma reads as the only living thing in the image.
