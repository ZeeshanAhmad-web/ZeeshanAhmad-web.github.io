Project

Bad Blood

Personal · Fan Art Spider-Man × Venom · Tribute Piece 2026

A tribute piece — Spider-Man cornered against a wall by a roaring symbiote, sky on fire behind them. Fan art; characters owned by Marvel.

A personal Illustration made out of love for the character and the genre. The composition was the entire point: the symbiote takes more than half the frame, the hero is pushed to the very edge of the canvas, and the sky is doing all the rest of the colour work.

Spider-Man and Venom are properties of Marvel Comics / Marvel Entertainment. This piece is fan art, made for personal practice and portfolio demonstration only — not for sale or commercial use.

Process

Thumbnailing — Initial ideation and composition planning.

Tight Sketch — Refining the anatomy, details, and linework.

Value Comps — Establishing the lighting and focal points.
