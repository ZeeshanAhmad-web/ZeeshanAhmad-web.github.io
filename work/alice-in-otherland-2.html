Project

Alice in Otherland II

Personal · Fan Art Reimagining · Reconnaissance 2025

Alice, recast as a scout in a castle of hearts — the queen’s court watched from the outside, by telescope.

The second piece in the Otherland series. Alice is no longer falling — she’s working. The castle in the distance carries the Queen’s heart banner; petals from the cherry tree drift past at the same rate the scene reveals itself.

Inspired by Lewis Carroll’s work, which is in the public domain. The reimagining is a personal study.

Process

Thumbnailing — Initial ideation and composition planning.

Tight Sketch — Refining the anatomy, details, and linework.

Value Comps — Establishing the lighting and focal points.

Color Comps — Exploring mood, palette, and atmosphere.
