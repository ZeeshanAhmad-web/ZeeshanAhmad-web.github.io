About

Zeeshan Ahmad.

I’m Zeeshan Ahmad, an Illustrator working in moody, narrative imagery for book and editorials. My work leans into atmosphere, shadow, texture, and the quiet tension between figure and space.

I’m currently an MFA candidate at SCAD Atlanta, and have been making Illustrations professionally since 2022. My practice sits at the intersection of fantasy, folklore, and literary fiction.
